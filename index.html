Three-Dimensional Visual Assurance of
Total Tumor Removal


TECHNIQUE

  1. Intact Specimen - 3D Scanned
  2. Entire specimen cross-sectioned and totally submitted for microscopic examination
  3. Each parallel cross-section oriented into consecutive layers
  4. Layers with tumor "inserted" into 3D Pathology Framework
  5. Distance from outer surface of tumor to outer surface of biopsy maintained

BENEFITS


  • Patient can see results first hand
  • Margin status is quantified - "ruler" for thickness of normal surrounding tissue
  • Surgeon better understands the completeness of the srugical removal
  • Improved surgical selection technique following incomplete removal
  • Malpractice defense
  • Selection for superior surgical skills
  • Surgical resident training
